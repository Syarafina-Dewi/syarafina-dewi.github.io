Implementation of Principal Component Analysis on K-Means for Clustering Education Levels Residents of Semarang Regency

Skills: Machine Learning Algorithm, Python, Analysis

Description:

In terms of the Gross Enrollment Ratio (GER) for higher education in Semarang Regency, it is still relatively low at 19.25%. Therefore, this research aims to classify the levels of education based on gender, age, and individual status within the family, using the K-Means algorithm and dimension reduction through the Principal Component Analysis (PCA) method.

This study is conducted by processing data from the March 2021 National Socio-Economic Survey (KOR) in the Semarang Regency, sourced from the Central Statistics Agency (BPS).

The results of my analysis yielded two main components with a cumulative proportion reaching 70%. Subsequently, after dimension reduction using the PCA method, cluster analysis is performed using the K-means algorithm, resulting in four clusters with different characteristics for each cluster.

Twitter Sentiment Analysis Towards Qatar as Host of the 2022 World Cup Using Textblob

Skills: Web Scraping, Textblob, Python

Description:

Twitter provides services to its users in the form of creating status messages or what are usually called tweets. Through tweets users can express opinions, views, or emotions on a topic.

On December 2, 2010, Qatar was selected to host the 2022 World Cup. The choice of Qatar to host the 2022 World Cup can elicit various responses from various groups around the world. This research uses Textblob to find out the sentiments of Twitter users around the world regarding Qatar as the host of the 2022 World Cup.

This research is divided into 3 stages, the first stage before Qatar was selected to host there was 88.46% positive sentiment and 11.54% negative sentiment, the second stage after Qatar was selected to host there was 79.38% positive sentiment and 20.62% negative sentiment, the third sentiment when the 2022 World Cup took place in Qatar was 83.72% positive sentiment and 16.28% negative sentiment. Based on the study, the model was able to accurately predict 83% of the total testing data. This study can predict new data without having to be labelled first. Read more

Skills: Machine Learning Algorithm, Rapid Miner, Analysis

Description:

Neuroticism refers to individual differences in emotional response, such as anxiety – a response to something that is threatening –, anger – an emotion that arises because of a disturbance that causes feelings of irritation, disappointment, and frustration –, depression – a mood disorder that causes deep feelings of sadness and indifference–, and vulnerability –psychological vulnerability or fragility–.

The subjects of this research is 86 students from various faculties and universities in Indonesia. This study aims to analyze students' mental health using the decision tree method
